Teuchi Udon

October 16, 2020

You will not be using your hands much.

Equipment:

  • Big food storage bag
  • Rolling pin
  • Muscles and a strong will

    • Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour + Cake flour
    All-purpose flour:
    50g

    Cake flour:
    50g
  • Bread flour + Cake flour
    Bread flour:
    25g

    Cake flour:
    75g
  • All-purpose flour + Corn starch
    All-purpose flour:
    90g

    Corn starch:
    10g
  • Bread flour + Corn starch
    Bread flour:
    75g

    Corn starch:
    25g
  • Water:
  • Salt:

    • Target protein content = 9%;
    Calculated using these protein contents: { all-purpose flour: 10%; Cake flour:8%; Bread flour: 12%; Corn starch: 0%; }

    Methods:

  • Mix all dry ingredients
  • Add water, mix, and form dough
  • Put dough in food storage bag
  • Step on bag, fold, repeat until mostly smooth
  • Let rest for 30 minutes
  • Shape into round ball, let rest for 2 hours
  • Dust with corn starch, roll dough out into rectangle, cut into noodles
  • Boil water, cook noodles (13-15 min), rinse and drain

    • How to use: Add to any of the following

  • Curry Udon

    Beef/Pork + dashi + curry roux + green onion

  • Beef Udon

    Beef + shoyu + dashi + mirin + sugar + green onion

  • Tanuki Udon

    Dashi + wakame + kamaboko + tenkasu + green onion

  • Kitsune Udon

    Dashi + sugar + mirin + shoyu + Inari-age(seasoned) + green onion

  • Miso Udon

    Dashi + mirin + miso + assorted veggies/meats/mushrooms/eggs

  • Yaki Udon

    Stir fry: pork, onion, cabbage, carrots, shitake, green onion, mentsuyu (dashi + mirin + sake + shoyu), in that order
